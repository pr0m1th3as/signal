Function Reference: zp2ss

Function File: [a, b, c, d] = zp2ss (z, p, k)

Conversion from zero / pole to state space.

Inputs

z
p

Vectors of (possibly) complex poles and zeros of a transfer function. Complex values must come in conjugate pairs (i.e., ×+jy in z means that ×-jy is also in z).

k

Real scalar (leading coefficient).

Outputs

a
b
c
d

The state space system, in the form: $$ \dot x = Ax + Bu $$$$ y = Cx + Du $$
